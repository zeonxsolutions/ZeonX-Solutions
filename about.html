Who We Are

An internship platform focused on building, not just watching

ZeonX Solutions provides project-based online internships across technology, design, analytics, and business domains. Students learn independently, complete domain-specific projects, submit work through GitHub, and receive feedback and verified documents based on completion.

Our objective is to make practical industry exposure accessible and affordable. The program does not replace formal employment or classroom education; it provides a structured environment for students to practise skills, document work, and improve their portfolio.

What every intern receives

  • Unique ZeonX Intern ID
  • Student dashboard access
  • GitHub project submission facility
  • Offer letter after payment confirmation
  • Project review and status tracking
  • Completion certificate after successful approval
  • Online Intern ID verification
